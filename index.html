About

I am currently a second-year undergraduate student in Bioinformatics (five-year program) at Harbin Medical University.

Currently, I am a visiting student at the Integrative Ecology Laboratory (ZIEL) at Nanjing University, conducting research on the application of causal inference in biodiversity under the guidance of Prof. Wenyuan Zhang.

My research interests span multiple fields, including philosophy (metaphysics, philosophy of religion), statistics (causal inference), and neuroscience — particularly the quantitative analysis of complex systems leading to exciting conclusions.

Beyond scientific research, I have a strong interest in contemporary popular culture, especially subcultures such as anime and manga, and previously served as a university researcher in the Cultural Observation division of Tencent IEG's Market and User Research Department (i-MUR).
